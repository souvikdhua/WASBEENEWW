Welcome to Wasabee

Kolkata's Favorite Standalone Pan-Asian Dining Destination

Established in 2012, Wasabee is proudly Kolkata's very first standalone restaurant to bring you authentic Japanese cuisine, alongside a rich menu of Burmese, Chinese, Singaporean, Malaysian, Korean, Indonesian, and Thai delicacies.

To give you the most authentic taste, we use high-quality imported ingredients like premium Norwegian salmon for our sushi. Everything is freshly prepared, and we are always happy to customize the spice levels and flavors to suit your Indian palate.

"Bon Appetit!"

Yours Truly,

Owner's Corner

"Building Kolkata's first standalone Japanese dining destination in 2012 wasn't just about food, it was about introducing a philosophy of pure, honest culinary art."

From importing genuine ingredients to crafting a space that feels like a quiet sanctuary, we wanted Wasabee to be a direct channel to Tokyo and Seoul. Our journey started over a decade ago with a simple mission: Zero convenience fees, zero delivery charges, honest direct pricing, and a culinary experience without any compromises.

Tanmoy Bose & Debraj Choudhury Founders of Wasabee
CUISINE
料理長

Chef's Corner

"We source premium Norwegian salmon daily and handcraft every single dumpling, ensuring authentic technique is alive in every plate."

Our kitchen runs on uncompromising principles: we refuse to use MSG (Ajinomoto) in any of our dishes to protect your health, and everything is prepared fresh from scratch. We embrace traditional Japanese precision while balancing spice levels to suit the local palate, turning every meal into a memorable encounter.

Master Chef & Team Culinary Directors
